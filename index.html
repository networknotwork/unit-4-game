Looter!

Your bag can only hold so many treasures, based on the random number assigned.


There are four different treasures of different weights. By clicking on one, you put one of that type into your bag.


If you can fill your bag without letting it overflow, you win!


Be wary, the weights of each treasure is initially unknown and they change each game.
